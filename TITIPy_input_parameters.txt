***************************************************************************************************
**********Input parameters for TITIPy (Topside Ionosphere Turbulence Indices with Python)**********
***************************************************************************************************
Y		Plotting of results (it requires additional time):  Y=yes, N=no
10		Width of the window used for RODI, ROTEI, and ROTI calculation (in seconds, integer)
1		TEC used for ROTI calculation: 1=absolute slant TEC   2=absolute vertical TEC   3=relative slant TEC
